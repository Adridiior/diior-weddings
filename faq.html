Got Questions?

FAQ

Do We Pick the Final Images?

The final selection is part of my creative process, I’ll take care of it for you. As a photographer, crafting a meaningful story through images means curating the gallery to reflect the full essence of your day, from start to finish. Every important moment will be there. If you decide to print an album or order prints, you’ll of course get to pick your favorites from the delivered gallery.

Is it necessary to take posed photos?
Or can we rely on something natural?

The most important thing is that you feel comfortable on the day of your event. We can be your artistic directors, choosing poses and moments together, or we can be silent guests, observing the unfolding of events and immortalizing them, capturing the naturalness and emotion of the moment.

How far in advance should we book?

As a first thing, let's reach out, let's have a talk in order to understand if we're right for each other. Ideally 6 to 12 months in advance would the be sweet spot, popular dates fill up quickly, so early booking ensures availability.

Do you adapt to the style or specific requests of the spouses?

Yes! It is essential to fully understand the couple's needs in order to comprehend their preferences, what motivates and excites them,
so I can truly work with them, not for them.
This allows me to deliver materials that reflect their vision and
create the photographs of their dreams.

When will we receive our photos?

The full gallery is usually ready within 4 to 6 weeks after your wedding day. If there are any particular needs to receive the photos more quickly it will be discussed privately.
